Authentication

Each request must be authenticated by the use of a token. To obtain your token, please contact your account manager at Netigate.

The token is provided with an HTTP header called X-API-KEY.

X-API-KEY: zdfh7hghjfj326hk2hk85h4k9gnm9v5bn
